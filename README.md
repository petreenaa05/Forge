Forge is a women-only digital platform designed to promote independence, confidence, and community-driven empowerment. The app connects women who possess practical, strength-based, or traditionally male-dominated skills (such as vehicle maintenance, weightlifting, repairs, or self-defense) with women who want to learn them through mentorship, skill exchange, and guided sessions.

In addition to skill-based matching, the platform includes a real-time community support space where users can post quick questions, share images or videos, and receive immediate advice from other members — creating a collaborative learning ecosystem.

The app also integrates a location-based assistance feature for urgent situations. In cases such as vehicle breakdowns or safety concerns, users can send a verified help request to nearby trusted members. Safety measures include identity verification, ratings and reviews, live location sharing, emergency contact alerts, and reporting systems to ensure responsible usage.

By combining mentorship, community knowledge-sharing, and real-time support, the platform builds a trusted women-led network that fosters skill development, safety, and mutual empowerment.
